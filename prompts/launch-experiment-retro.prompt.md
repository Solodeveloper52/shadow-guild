# launch-experiment-retro

Given launch experiment context and results, produce:

- Hypothesis vs observed outcome
- Metric deltas and confidence notes
- Root causes for wins and misses
- Decisions to keep, stop, or scale
- Next experiment backlog with priority

## Deep Prompt Contract

### Required Inputs

- Objective, constraints, baseline evidence, and audience.

### Required Outputs

- Prioritized summary, detailed analysis, risks, and next actions.

### Quality Rules

- Label assumptions, tie recommendations to evidence, include fallback.
